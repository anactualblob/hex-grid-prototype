Hex Grid Prototype

This is a prototype of a hex grid I threw together in a week. You can move the player by clicking on it and clicking on a hex in its range. The colors don't mean anything.

The grid is constructed by passing a text file to the program that looks like this. Each character corresponds to a hex on the grid.


This is very buggy. Known bugs include :

  • Going left or up will teleport the player instead of moving smoothly.
  • You can click outside of the grid and the player will move towards there and never stop.
  • Going up and right will make the player move infinitely.

I won't be fixing these bugs, since I gave myself a week to complete this and the week has now ended. It was meant as an exercice/challenge in programming.
